## How to Install

1. Download the latest version of CoinTracker for macOS.  
2. Open the DMG installer and move CoinTracker.app to the Applications folder.  
3. Launch the app and allow it through macOS Gatekeeper if required.

## Overview

<a href="#">CoinTracker for macOS</a> is built to function as a central hub for crypto portfolio understanding across multiple exchanges, wallets and networks. Instead of relying on scattered interfaces and ad hoc spreadsheets, users gain a single, continuous view of balances, allocations and performance over time. The design emphasizes calm, readable layouts that make it easy to inspect both current positions and historical context. This approach reduces the friction of staying organized while trading actively or investing for the long term. As records accumulate, CoinTracker becomes a living archive of the userâ€™s financial journey in digital assets.

The syncing engine connects to major platforms and ingests new transactions with minimal user effort. Deposits, withdrawals, swaps and transfers are categorized using structured logic that aims to minimize manual cleanup. When anomalies occur, CoinTracker presents them clearly rather than burying them, allowing users to correct issues through guided editing rather than raw data manipulation. These <a href="#">reconciliation workflows</a> are especially important for users with multi-year trading histories, where missing a handful of events could distort the entire picture. Over time, the portfolio becomes cleaner, more accurate and easier to audit.

Tax tooling is another anchor of the CoinTracker experience. The app computes gains, losses and cost basis across connected accounts and packages the results into exportable summaries. This reduces the burden of preparing regulatory documentation and makes it easier to collaborate with accountants or financial advisors. Users who trade frequently benefit from having a continuously updated view of taxable outcomes instead of waiting until the end of the year. With macOS integration, these processes feel like part of a broader, well-structured financial routine rather than a last-minute scramble.

Visual elements in the macOS edition are tuned for long sessions of review and planning. Charts, allocation diagrams and trend lines aim for clarity over spectacle, helping users see meaningful patterns in portfolio evolution. Filters for time ranges, asset groups and platforms allow for focused analysis without losing the bigger picture. As habits form around checking CoinTracker, users develop a more grounded sense of risk, diversification and long-term performance, turning raw transaction streams into actionable insight.
